## Corrections Applied

### CE-01: Space before closing quotation mark in interrupted dialogue

**Location:** Line 50

[CE: `*"Don't — "*` → `*"Don't —"*`]

A space appeared between the em dash and the closing quotation mark. Per CMOS 6.87, no space precedes the closing quotation mark in interrupted dialogue. The em dash itself already signals the break; the space is erroneous. Corrected silently.

**Before:** `*"Don't try too hard to think,"* he said. *"Don't — "*[^staring]`
**After:** `*"Don't try too hard to think,"* he said. *"Don't —"*[^staring]`
